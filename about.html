< – – Hi, I'm Lavanya Kumar, and this is my story...

Based on my experience as a designer as well as my interaction with day to day items throughout my life, I have learned that users ignore designs that ignore the user. Today, practicing user-centered design has become the focal point of several industries, and I am excited to be a part of this movement. I had always had an interest in front-end development, specifically HTML and CSS, and soon learned the strength of its application in the realm of user interface design. I was introduced to Cognitive Science at UC San Diego, specializing in Human Computer Interaction and am a proud graduate of the department three years later. Since then I have continued to polish my skills through online courses centered around design and programming. I also enjoy working with Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator and have completed several projects editing live traces with the software as well as used the tools for prototyping and wire-framing purposes (along with other tools such as Axure and GoMockingbird). Currently, I am pursuing my masters in Information Science at the University of Michigan's School of Information. Not only will I continue my study of Human Computer Interaction, but I hope to learn more about and apply my knowledge of design to the area of Information Analysis and Retrieval.

Some of my passions outside of HCI include eating, travelling, and singing. I am a huge fan of a cappella, and have participated in several competitions and showcases throughout college! I also love yoga and working out, however those activities tend to become compensated by my interest in food (Click for Food Blog). Please feel free to view my LinkedIn and Resume, explore my portfolio, and contact me for any questions/thoughts/suggestions!
